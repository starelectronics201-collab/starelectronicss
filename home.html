Home Appliance Repair

At Star Electronics, we specialize in repairing a wide range of home appliances. From daily-use gadgets to major machines, our expert technicians provide fast, reliable, and long-lasting service at your doorstep.

Microwave Repair

Expert repair for all brands of microwave ovens. We fix heating issues, display problems, door or button malfunctions, and ensure safe and efficient operation. Our team also provides cleaning and maintenance tips to prolong your microwave's life.

Kitchen Hood (Chimney) Repair

Professional repair for kitchen chimneys. We clean and fix suction, motor, and electrical issues. Ensure proper ventilation in your kitchen while removing grease, smoke, and odors efficiently. Regular maintenance services available for long-term performance.

Washing Machine Repair

Professional repair for all washing machine brands. We handle drum, motor, electrical, and water leakage issues for perfect cleaning performance. Our team also provides installation checks, detergent guidance, and preventive maintenance tips.
